Villa Design

"Architecture is the learned game, correct and magnificent, of forms assembled in the light."

Three private residences, three landscapes — each villa shaped by the silent rhythm of its own site.

PROJECT 01 — PORTUGAL

Aroeira Villa

Set against a pine forest and a still lily pond, Aroeira Villa lets a single sculptural roofline tie its two wings together — a private sanctuary where the wave of white concrete frames the water below.

PROJECT 02 — AZERBAIJAN

Narenj Villa

Sharp folded planes in pale stucco and dark timber meet a marble-floored interior of restrained luxury — a study in geometric confidence set within a quiet residential plot.

PROJECT 03 — UNITED ARAB EMIRATES

Layers Villa

Stacked horizontal planes and a glowing glass volume frame the pool at dusk — a villa designed to be experienced after dark, where warm interior light becomes the architecture's main material.
